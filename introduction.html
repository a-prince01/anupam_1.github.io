Why mounting is required?

Check the sizes of specimens you may need to analyse

   •Sometimes it is large

   •Sometimes it is very small to hold in hand

 →The large samples need to be cut so that it can be gripped

 →Small samples need to be clipped to polish the desired face

The purpose of mounting is to protect fragile or coated materials during preparation and to obtain perfect edge retention. Mounting is used when the protection of layers is imperative, and also it enables a safer and more convenient handling of small, sharp, or irregularly shaped specimens.
